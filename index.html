About

Who we are

Founded in 2002, Lintechnokrats remains committed to delivering expert IT support services and web application development to organizations by leveraging Open Source technologies. Today, we continue to understand and address the comprehensive IT support, development, and web application needs of our clients, ensuring that our solutions are both technically sound and cost-effective. Our unwavering dedication to our clients positions us as a trusted technology partner, seamlessly integrating with their IT teams.

With years of industry experience, the Lintechnokrats team effectively aligns Open Source technologies with diverse business models, providing a competitive edge in the technology space. Serving clients across various sectors, we offer tailored support and web application development options to meet their specific needs. Our knowledge, experience, and access to the latest technological advancements ensure we remain a reliable and valuable partner for our customers.

Our mission is to collaborate with our clients in developing solutions and support strategies that effectively and efficiently meet their business objectives, while ensuring practicality, sustainability, and a clear return on investment.
